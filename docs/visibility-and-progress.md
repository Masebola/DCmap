# Visibility and progress

## Route views

- Reading List: working route, with completed entries hidden when auto-hide is enabled
- All: every included entry
- Completed: only fully completed entries

## Optional reading

Optional entries are stored in the structured route and can be toggled. They have separate progress and do not prevent an era reaching 100% main-route completion.

## Completed entries

Ordinary completed entries may disappear from the Reading List. They remain visible under All and Completed Library. Completed events collapse to preserve the phase skeleton.

## Active lanes

Each structured era stores its own active route IDs. Select All and Clear All affect visibility only and never alter progress.

## Outside the Roadmap

Excluded, skimmed and detached titles are informational. They do not affect canonical route totals or event prerequisites.
